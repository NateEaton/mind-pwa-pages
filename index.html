What is the MIND Diet Tracker?

The MIND Diet Tracker is a simple, free tool that helps you follow the MIND Diet (Mediterranean-DASH Intervention for Neurodegenerative Delay) - an eating pattern shown to support brain health and reduce cognitive decline risk.

Try It Now

Works on any device with a modern web browser. For the best experience, use the "Add to Home Screen" option on your device to install it like a regular app.
